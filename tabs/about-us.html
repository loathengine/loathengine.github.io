Welcome to Empirical Precision

Empirical Precision is designed to help you organize your reloading data, track your firearms, and most importantly, analyze your target performance with statistical rigor.

Key Feature: Composite Analysis

Stop worrying about shooting large 10-20 shot groups in one sitting. With Empirical Precision, you can shoot multiple smaller groups (e.g., 3 or 5 shots) across different targets or days. In the Analysis Tab, you can select these separate sessions to composite them into a single aggregate group. This gives you high-quality statistical data (Mean Radius, SD) without the fatigue or mirage of long strings of fire.

Privacy First

This application runs 100% offline in your browser. All your data—firearms, recipes, target images, and analysis—is stored locally on your device. Nothing is ever uploaded to a server.

Recommended Workflow

  1. Setup: Use the Misc and Components tabs to populate your bullets, powders, and equipment.
  2. Create Data: Add your Firearms and create Load Data recipes.
  3. Upload: Go to the Targets tab to upload images of your range results.
  4. Mark: Use the Marking tab to set the scale and mark impacts on your targets. Save each group as a session.
  5. Analyze: Go to the Analysis tab. Select multiple sessions for the same load to see the composite performance.
