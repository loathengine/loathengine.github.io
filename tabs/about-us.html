Empirical Precision

Empirical Precision is a comprehensive ballistic analysis and reloading management suite designed for precision shooters who demand statistical rigor. This application facilitates the meticulous organization of reloading components, firearm tracking, and advanced target analysis.

Recommended Workflow

  1. Add Firearm: Define your rifle or pistol in the Firearms tab.
  2. Add Load Data: Log your commercial ammo or custom handload recipe.
  3. Create Target: Use the Target generator to design your ideal target.
  4. Print Target: Print your generated custom target.
  5. Range Session: Take the target to the range and shoot your groups (make impacts on the target).
  6. Import Target: Take a photo of your shot target and import it as an image into the app.
  7. Tag Target: Use the "Tag Target" tool in the Targets gallery to invisibly link the image to the Firearm and Load data you used. This eliminates tedious data entry and complex naming conventions.
  8. Mark Shots: Load the tagged target in the Marking tab. The app will automatically sync your Firearm and Load data. Set the scale, mark your shots, and save the session.
  9. Analyze Session: Run statistical analysis on the range session target. The analysis engine relies on these ID tags rather than string parsing, guaranteeing accurate load tracking.
  10. Compare Sessions: Select multiple sessions to composite and compare your data.
  11. Check Stability: If necessary, use the Stability tab to check the gyroscopic stability of your bullet.

Scientific Methodology

Traditional methods of evaluating load performance often suffer from small sample sizes. Empirical Precision leverages composite group analysis, allowing users to aggregate multiple smaller sample groups (e.g., several 3 or 5-shot groups) into a single, statistically significant dataset by aligning their Centers of Impact or Points of Aim. This approach prioritizes metrics like Mean Radius over extreme spread (group size), which is highly susceptible to outliers, mitigating the noise inherent in limited testing and providing a much more accurate representation of true system dispersion.

Comparative Analytics

Move beyond anecdotal evidence. The Analysis Module enables direct, side-by-side comparison of different load developments, seating depths, or tuner settings. By evaluating key metrics such as Mean Radius, Standard Deviation, and the 95th Percentile Spread (the statistically rigorous diameter guaranteed to contain 95% of future shots) across aggregated datasets, users can make data-driven decisions to optimize their precision.

Gyroscopic Stability Calculator

The application features a built-in gyroscopic stability ($S_g$) calculator utilizing the Refined Miller Twist Rule. It automatically applies effective length corrections for plastic-tipped bullets and dynamically models the Air Density Ratio (ADR) relative to sea-level based on environmental parameters like pressure (station vs. sea-level), temperature, altitude, and current velocity.

Privacy & Architecture

Built with a privacy-first architecture, Empirical Precision operates as a fully offline client-side application. All data—such as rifle info, proprietary load recipes, and target imagery—is stored entirely locally within your browser's IndexedDB. No data is ever transmitted to external servers.
