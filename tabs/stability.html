Understanding Gyroscopic Stability (Sg)

  • Sg < 1.0: Unstable. Bullet will tumble.
  • 1.0 < Sg < 1.5: Marginally Stable. Risk of inaccuracy and reduced BC.
  • Sg > 1.5: Fully Stable. Recommended for long-range precision.
  • Sg > 2.0: Over-stabilized. Small imperfections in bullet balance may cause increased dispersion, but the bullet is flight-stable.

Note: This calculator uses the Miller Twist Rule. It is an estimation and results may vary based on specific bullet geometry.
