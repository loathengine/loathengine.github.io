Definitions

Mean Radius (MR)
The average distance of all shots from the center of the group (Mean Point of Impact). A statistically robust measure of precision.
95th Percentile Radius
The estimated radius of a circle centered on the group that would contain 95% of future shots. Derived from Mean Radius.
MR Confidence (95%)
The 95% confidence interval for the true population Mean Radius, calculated via bootstrapping. Indicates the reliability of the observed Mean Radius.
Group Size
The maximum distance between the centers of the two furthest shots in the group (Extreme Spread).
A-ZED (A-Zone Effective Distance)
The theoretical maximum distance where 95% of shots would land within the width of an IPSC A-zone (15cm), based on the group's precision.
Horizontal SD
Standard Deviation of shot positions along the horizontal (X) axis.
Vertical SD
Standard Deviation of shot positions along the vertical (Y) axis.
Velocity SD
Standard Deviation of the recorded muzzle velocities.
Dispersion Analysis
Analyzes the relationship between vertical dispersion and velocity variation to detect potential tuning issues.
